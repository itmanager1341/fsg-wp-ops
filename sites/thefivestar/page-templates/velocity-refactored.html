Who Belongs at Velocity

FORCE Members

The credentialed core of the community. FORCE-certified agents and brokers built this over 15 years. Velocity is your home event. FORCE Connect gives you direct, structured time with the institutional professionals who attend as guests of the association.

Agents, Brokers, and Investors

Real estate professionals building businesses in REO, distressed assets, and single-family residential investment. If your career touches institutional inventory or alternative transaction structures, this is your professional community.

Asset Managers and Disposition Teams

Servicer-side and third-party asset managers overseeing REO portfolios and distressed disposition. Velocity is built around the practitioner network you rely on. Structured access to credentialed professionals is built into the program.

What Happens at Velocity

FORCE Connect

Credentialed agents sit down with the asset managers and disposition professionals who attend as guests of the association. Not a reception. A structured format built for direct professional introductions between practitioners and the institutional professionals who manage REO portfolios.

Asset Manager Panel

Servicer-side professionals on stage discussing how they build and manage their practitioner networks. Direct audience Q&A.

FSI Offers Platform Launch

Live demonstration and on-stage signup. Cash offer aggregation, website visitor intelligence, property marketing tools, and skip trace. The free tier is open to anyone. Paid membership unlocks the full platform.

Four General Sessions and Two Breakouts

Covering REO, SFR, fix-and-flip, tech you can use in your business, and alternative revenue strategies.

Five Star Alliance Charter Member Offer

The first 100 new Five Star Alliance members signed at Velocity receive Charter Member pricing. On-stage signup.

Networking and Social Events

Two evenings of structured social programming built around the community.
