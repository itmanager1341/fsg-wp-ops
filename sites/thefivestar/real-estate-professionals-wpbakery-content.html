Real Estate Professionals

Your path into the Five Star Institute real estate professional community.

The agents and brokers who work REO, distressed property, and institutional real estate operate in a market that looks nothing like conventional residential. The relationships are longer to build. The transactions are more complex. And the professionals on the other side of every assignment have their own standards for who they work with.

Five Star Institute's real estate professional community exists because this market requires its own infrastructure: a credentialing system that asset managers and servicers recognize, events where the institutional professionals who control inventory actually participate, and membership benefits built specifically for how this business works.

Founding Institutional Partner

Free, Application-Based

Asset managers, servicer-side disposition teams, institutional SFR operators, GSE and HUD professionals, and NPL fund managers are eligible for Founding Institutional Partner status through December 31, 2026.

Founding Institutional Partners receive Five Star Alliance benefits at no cost, are named in the FSI RE community directory, recognized in event materials, and featured in MortgagePoint's RE community coverage.

This is not a free pass. It is a founding role in the professional network that serves your portfolio. The quality of practitioners you work with is directly shaped by the standards this community sets, and Founding Institutional Partners help set them.

Governance

The FSI real estate professional community is governed by a practitioner-led Advisory Council drawn from active FORCE and community members. The Advisory Council shapes event programming, FORCE credentialing standards, and community direction. The Advisory Council Chair holds a representative seat on the Five Star Executive Council, FSI's unified governing body.

This is the same governance model that drives every FSI professional community. Practitioners lead. The association serves.
