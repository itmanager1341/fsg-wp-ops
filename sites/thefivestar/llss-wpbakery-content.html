Join Legal League

The Legal League Servicer Summit is where the financial services bar and the mortgage servicing industry sit down together. A full day of education, direct dialogue, and the professional relationships that define how servicers and their counsel navigate every stage of the housing lifecycle.

Programming is shaped by the Legal League Advisory Council and built for how attorneys, firm leaders, and servicer legal teams actually work. The Summit is intimate by design: small enough for substantive conversation, large enough for the full industry to be in the room.

Who Belongs at the Summit

Legal League Member Firms

The credentialed core of the financial services bar. Legal League member firms are the practices that represent servicers, GSEs, and financial institutions at every stage of the housing lifecycle. The Summit is your community's home event, the room your Advisory Council programs, and the platform your firm's work is built into.

Financial Services Attorneys

Foreclosure, bankruptcy, default, and mortgage servicing attorneys building practices around the servicing industry. If your docket touches the full default lifecycle, this is the community and the event built for how you work.

Mortgage Servicers and In-House Counsel

Servicer legal, compliance, and default operations teams attend the Summit as guests of the association. The program is designed around the issues that land on your desk and the outside counsel you depend on to resolve them.

What Happens at the Summit

A single-day program, purpose-built for practitioners. You will hear from mortgage servicers, GSEs, and government officials speaking into the issues driving the industry. Each session is shaped by the Legal League Advisory Council and scoped for working attorneys who need takeaways they can apply on Monday morning.

Industry Leadership Panel

Mortgage servicers, GSE representatives, and government officials on stage together, working through the policy, operational, and legal issues driving the industry. The signature session of the Summit.

CLE Programming

Continuing legal education credits delivered through Advisory Council-shaped sessions. Case law intensives, procedural updates, and practice-area content for attorneys actively practicing servicer-side.

Regulatory and Policy Briefings

The year ahead in federal and state default policy. Sessions covering CFPB activity, state-level legislation, court procedural changes, and the regulatory climate shaping servicer and firm practice.

Advisory Council Forum

Open session with the Legal League Advisory Council. Member firm priorities, programming direction for the year ahead, and the industry positions Legal League carries into its regulatory and advocacy work.

Closing Reception

The day closes with open networking built around the Legal League member community. The room every attorney in this practice area wants to be in once a year.
